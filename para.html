<p>
Dum Biryani is a very popular and tasty Indian dish. It is made with rice, meat, and special spices. The word “Dum” means slow cooking in steam. In this method, the food is cooked on low flame for a long time. This helps all the flavors mix well. The rice becomes soft and full of aroma. Chicken or mutton is usually used in Dum Biryani. It is cooked in a sealed pot to keep the taste inside. Dum Biryani is famous for its rich smell and delicious taste.
